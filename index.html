1. SCOPE OF SERVICES
The Service Provider agrees to provide AI workflow automation services to the Client, including but not limited to: design, development, implementation, and maintenance of automated workflows using AI technologies, machine learning models, APIs, and related software solutions. 2.1 Client Ownership of Workflows
Upon full payment of all fees due, the Client shall own all rights, title, and interest in and to the custom AI workflows, configurations, and specific implementations created exclusively for the Client under this Agreement. Any third-party software, APIs, or services integrated into the workflows remain subject to their respective license terms. The Service Provider warrants that all third-party components used are properly licensed for commercial use.
3. FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS
3.1 Service Fees
The Client agrees to pay the Service Provider fees as specified in the applicable SOW. Unless otherwise stated, fees are quoted in USD and are exclusive of all applicable taxes.

6.3 Termination for Cause

6.4 Effects of Termination
